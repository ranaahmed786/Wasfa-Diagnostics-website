About Us

At Wasfa Diagnostic Centre, we are committed to providing reliable and accurate diagnostic services to help our patients maintain their health. Our team of experts uses the latest equipment and technology to ensure the highest standards of care.

Our Services

Open MRI (GE USA)

Our advanced Open MRI system from GE USA offers a comfortable and spacious scanning experience without compromising diagnostic precision. Ideal for patients who are claustrophobic or require extra space, it delivers high-quality imaging with state-of-the-art technology for accurate and reliable results.

High-Speed 16 Slice CT Scan

Our High-Speed 16-Slice CT Scan provides rapid, detailed imaging with exceptional clarity. This advanced technology enables precise diagnostics with reduced scanning time, ensuring patient comfort and accurate results for a wide range of medical conditions.

Mammography

Our state-of-the-art mammography services ensure accurate and early detection of breast abnormalities. With advanced imaging technology, we provide high-quality scans in a comfortable and supportive environment, prioritizing your health and peace of mind.

C.R Digital X-Ray(Plain and Contrast)

Our C.R Digital X-Ray services utilize advanced technology to deliver high-resolution images for precise diagnosis. Offering both plain and contrast imaging, we ensure accurate results with reduced radiation exposure in a patient-friendly environment.

Ultrasonography(Colour Doppler and Interventional)

Our advanced ultrasonography services, including color Doppler and interventional procedures, provide detailed imaging for accurate diagnosis and treatment. With cutting-edge technology and skilled professionals, we ensure precision and patient comfort in every scan.

Pathology Lab(Hormones and Body Fluids)

Our fully equipped pathology lab offers comprehensive testing for hormones and body fluids. With precise analysis and modern equipment, we ensure accurate results to support effective diagnosis and treatment.
